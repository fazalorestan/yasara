# YaSara v1.0 to v1.1 Roadmap

## v1.0 Status

YaSara Professional v1.0 is stable, clean-packaged, and safe by default.

## v1.1 Goals

- Production-grade exchange connectivity
- Real WebSocket streaming
- Account/order/position sync
- Stronger risk controls
- Safety-gated live trading
- Better desktop packaging and installer automation

## Safety Rule

Live trading must remain disabled until all production safety gates pass.
